The Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) 5.0 is a government-backed credit initiative designed to help MSMEs, businesses, and airlines navigate short-term liquidity challenges. It provides collateral-free additional working capital with up to a 100% guarantee from the National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company (NCGTC).Key FeaturesEligibility: Open to business enterprises and MSMEs whose existing credit facilities were categorized as 'standard' (excluding SMA-2) as of March 31, 2026.Loan Amounts:MSMEs / Non-MSMEs: Up to 20% of peak fund-based working capital used during Q4 FY2026 (Jan–Mar 2026), capped at ₹100 crore per borrower.Airlines: Up to 100% of peak credit outstanding, capped at ₹1,500 crore per borrower.Interest Rates:Banks: MSMEs at EBLR + 0.75% and Non-MSMEs at MCLR + 0.75% (capped at 9% per annum).NBFCs: Capped at 13% per annum.Tenure: 5 years for MSMEs/Non-MSMEs (including a 1-year moratorium), and 7 years for airlines (including a 2-year moratorium).Fees: Zero processing fees, prepayment penalties, or guarantee fees.How to ApplyApplications must be routed mandatorily through the official JanSamarth Portal. The scheme remains available for loans sanctioned up to March 31, 2027. You can view the full onboarding partners and application guidelines on the NCGTC Portal.
